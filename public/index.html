JMB Basements is a family-run finishing crew based in Auburn Hills, Michigan. We focus exclusively on basements, which means every project gets the planning, materials, and attention to detail it deserves.

From a single-room rec area to a full lower-level buildout with a bathroom and wet bar, we handle the whole job — demo, framing, electrical coordination, drywall, flooring, trim, and paint.
